Reading the shift

MDP won in 2019, lost the seat to PNC in 2024, then retook it in the 2026 by-election. The seat became more competitive over time. The winning margin fell from 423 votes in 2019 to 62 votes in 2026.

Eligible voters increased from 3,849 to 4,121, while turnout fell from 75.37% to 66.25%.
